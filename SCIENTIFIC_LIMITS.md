# Scientific Limits

Azuero Kairós classifies Sentinel observations into confidence states for cautious agricultural decision support.

The system does not detect pesticides, atrazine, pathogens, heavy metals, dissolved chemical contamination, or safe water.

The system does not declare whether water is safe for drinking, irrigation, livestock, or human contact.

The decision states are:

- `usable`
- `low_confidence`
- `do_not_infer`

When evidence quality is insufficient, the correct output is `do_not_infer`.
