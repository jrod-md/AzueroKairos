# Compliance and Originality

This repository is the official hackathon build of Azuero Kairós.

Pre-hackathon work was limited to planning, scope definition, and discarded feasibility spikes. Those spike outputs are not official deliverables and must not be copied into this build.

Official code, official results, official dashboards, official briefs, and official submission materials are generated during the hackathon window from this repository.

The project uses Copernicus Earth Observation data to support a satellite confidence decision layer for agricultural decision support in Azuero, Panama. It does not claim to detect contamination or declare water safe.
